About Us

Who are we?

We are a small group of game developers within the SoftwareSS foundation with diverse experience in game development. In spite of the fact that we have a limited number of members, we make games which are unique through our creativity.

Who do we do?

We make simple 2D and 3D games for fun, but we add some creative spice and turn them into something that people will be excited about.

How do we do it?

Our games are created using the Unity engine and occasionally the Unreal Engine. We create 3D models with Blender. Due to our limited member count, we use resources from the web to fill in the gaps we can't fill.
