About us

Who are we?


We are a small group of game developers within the SoftwareSS foundation with diverse game development experience. The games we make are limited due to our small size, but we use creativity to make them special.



What do we do?


We make simple 2D and 3D games for fun, but we add some creative spice and turn them into something that people will be excited about.



How do we do it?


We use the Unity engine and sometimes the Unreal engine to create our games. We use Blender to create 3d models, but due to our limited size, we use resources from the web to fill in the gaps we can't fill.
